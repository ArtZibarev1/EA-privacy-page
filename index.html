Privacy Policy for Adveng

Thank you for using Adveng! We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how your data is handled.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Adveng does not collect, store, or transmit user-related data to our servers or third-party services. However, the Yandex Mobile Ads SDK may process certain technical data to deliver relevant ads and improve advertising services. This data includes:

  • Device Identifiers: e.g., advertising identifier (IDFA) for iOS.
  • Technical Device Data: device model, OS version, network info.
  • App Usage Data: interactions with the app and ads.

Yandex processes this data in compliance with their privacy policy and does not share it with Adveng or other entities.

2. Data Sharing

Adveng does not share user data. All data processed by the Yandex SDK is managed independently by Yandex under their privacy policy.

3. Security

Yandex Mobile Ads SDK employs industry-standard security measures. Adveng ensures compliance with App Store policies and best practices during SDK integration.

4. Your Privacy Choices

As a user, you can control app tracking and ad personalization:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy > Tracking to manage permissions.
  • Reset your advertising identifier to minimize ad personalization.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy due to app changes, legal requirements, or Yandex's practices. Updates will be posted here with a revised effective date.
